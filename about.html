About me

I’m Victoria (or Vivi if we're close :3). I consider myself a chronically online person with too many feelings about early-2000s rock, lo-fi confessions, and albums that sound like the soundtrack to my self induced insomnia. This site is basically my digital record bin; a place to keep track of what I own, what I want, and how each piece makes my brain feel slightly more prone to not kidnapping all my serotonin.

About the project

This archive was built as a pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript project, inspired by old Geocities fan pages, early tech blogs, and the feeling of wandering into a dim, half-organized record store.

It uses responsive layouts (phone, tablet, desktop), CSS Grid for the shelves, and a few interactive scripts to filter, sort, and browse different formats.

Tech & design notes

  • Built with semantic HTML and accessible navigation.
  • CSS Grid powers the album shelves and homepage tiles.
  • Three breakpoints: phone, tablet, and desktop.
  • Mostly just vibinh with hand-rolled front-end.
