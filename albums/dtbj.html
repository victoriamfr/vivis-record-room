Dedicated to Bobby Jameson

Ariel Pink · Mexican Summer · 2017

file under: dream pop, nostalgic, haunted.

Format
CD
Pressing
US Mexican Summer · standard edition
Condition
Tray a bit scratched, booklet still crisp
Purchased
Online order during a weird late-night Ariel spiral, 2020s
Filed under
dream pop · nostalgic · haunted

Rating: 4.7 / 5

feels like walking around a ghost town in the late afternoon, everything washed-out and bittersweet. probably his most quietly emotional one for me.

Album notes

This is the one that makes the “joke” part of his music feel less like a joke. it’s still murky and strange, but there’s a real, tired kind of grief humming underneath the gloss.

I tend to put it on when I’m stuck thinking about people who kind of vanished mid-story – the ones who left blogs and posts and traces behind. it sounds like internet ephemera turned into a faded shrine.
