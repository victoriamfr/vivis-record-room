Album notes

It’s one of the most “classic Ariel” feeling records to me, it's catchy, weird, dreamy, and slightly nauseous in a perfect way. everything sounds like it’s been re-recorded over old tapes, with ghosts of other songs bleeding through.

alisa is the perfect song to put on repeat like, 50 times, and cry and whine and scream and beg and bam your brain is melted and you're in my heart, you're in my dreams, you're in my soul.
