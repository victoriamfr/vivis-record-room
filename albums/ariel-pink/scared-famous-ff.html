Rating: 4.4 / 5

feels like digging through a shoebox of cursed demos in someone’s basement. overwhelming, it's weirdly intimate.

Album notes

This is the “too much” record – there’s just an avalanche of songs, ideas, fragments, and half-remembered hooks. it sounds like a radio station possessed by one very specific guy’s subconscious. when this song.. became... my favorite song.....

it's the one I play most often, a way to fully drown in his world, this is the one I reach for. you don’t listen to it as much as live inside it for an hour and obsess over the lyricism.
