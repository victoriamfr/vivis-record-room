Scared Famous / FF

Ariel Pink · Human Ear · 2007

file under: lo-fi, cult classic, everything held together with tape.

Format
CD
Pressing
Human Ear Music · double album
Condition
Scuffs on the case, audio already sounds broken in (by design)
Purchased
Picked up online after realizing i was severely obsessed with the song “the list”
Filed under
lo-fi · cult classic · early Ariel chaos

Rating: 4.8 / 5

feels like digging through a shoebox of cursed demos in someone’s basement. overwhelming, it's weirdly intimate.

Album notes

This is the “too much” record – an avalanche of songs, ideas, fragments, and half-remembered hooks. it sounds like a radio station possessed by one very specific guy’s subconscious. when this song.. became... my favorite song.....

it's the one I play most often — a way to fully drown in his world. you don’t listen to it as much as live inside it for an hour and obsess over the lyricism.
