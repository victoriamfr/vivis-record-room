Rating: 4.6 / 5

this is the “am I actually losing it or is it just the record” album. chaotic, smeared, and weirdly comforting if you’re in the right headspace.

Album notes

On vinyl it really does feel like some cursed VHS soundtrack got pressed straight to wax. the songs blur into each other like channel surfing in a trance. also, CREDIT CREDIT CREDIT

I don’t put this on casually, it’s more of a “let’s lean into the brain fog and see what happens” kind of listen. but when it hits, it hits in a way no other record can.
