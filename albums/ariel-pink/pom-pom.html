Feels like getting dizzy in the candy aisle at 3pm and then suddenly realizing the candy is looking back at you. perfect.

Album notes

This one is like my “brain has too many tabs open” record. Everything is neon and sticky and a little cursed, but somehow there’s real heartbreak peeking through the cartoon voices.

I reach for it when I’m fried and need something maximal and ridiculous that still feels oddly sincere. half clown nightmare, half secretly tender pop record.
