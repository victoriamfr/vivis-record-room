Album notes

i love this album, it feels crooked in all the right ways. every song is like a channel flip between decades, held together by this sticky, anxious warmth.

I use it as a bridge record when I want to show people what I like without completely terrifying them yet. accessible enough, but still full of strange little ghosts. really good way to ease into Ariel Pink imo.
