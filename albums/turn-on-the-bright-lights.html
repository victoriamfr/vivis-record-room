Rating: 5.0 / 5

Core memory record. Feels like standing under a lone streetlight at 2am while everything important quietly falls apart.

Album notes

This is basically the spine of my collection. Guitars like neon cutting through fog, vocals that sound detached until you listen too closely and realize they’re falling apart a little. It also is the soundtrack of every single one of my harshest heartbreaks, which is probably unhealthy but also whatever.

Favorite listens: late at night with headphones, back home when I still had a balcony, in the weird suppl closet/bathroom staring at the snowy view.
