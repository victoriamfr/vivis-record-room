[GIG LOG] Interpol — Turn On the Bright Lights in full

Oct 14, 2025 · Ann Arbor, MI · Small club w/ too many strobe lights

Posted by vivi · status: not okay but vibing

Setlist felt like my high school brain opened up and walked onstage. Sound was a little muddy but the guitars still cut through like neon in fog. Crowd was weirdly polite. 10/10 would lose my voice again.

[SHOW REPORT] Elliott Smith tribute night

Sep 2, 2025 · Detroit, MI · Tiny bar w/ christmas lights

Posted by vivi · mood: beautifully unwell

Local bands covering Elliott with varying levels of emotional damage. Some songs were rough, some were perfect by accident. Everyone sang along to “Between the Bars” and it felt like a group therapy session that no one paid for.

[LIVE] Wilco — everything hurts but it’s fine

Nov 5, 2024 · Chicago, IL · Theater w/ broken seats

Posted by vivi · tag: heartache with static

“Jesus, Etc.” live is basically emotional vandalism. Band sounded too tight for how destroyed everyone in the crowd looked. Left with a blurry phone video and the urge to reorganize my entire life.
